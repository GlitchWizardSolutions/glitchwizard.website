How We Help

We do this for FREE for Indiana families, because we are better together.

Plans to Keep your House

We make a plan just for YOU to help you keep your home. We give you the tools you need to help you find your way and hold your hand through it all.

Help Moving

Moving is hard. We can help you find a good and safe new home. We can help with movers, boxes, and a moving truck too! Let us carry that burden for you!

Help Talking to your Bank

Is your bank hard to talk to? We talk to the bank WITH you. We know how the banks work. We help you ask the right questions to get you answers. Unlike the bank, we are on YOUR side.

Help with Bills

Struggling to keep up with all your bills? We help you find local groups that can help catch up your late bills for water, gas, and electricity.

Help Sell your House

We offer different options to sell your house and show you why each option is good and bad, to help you find the best one for you.

Help with Food

Are you hungry? We help you get food for your family. We help you find a hot meal because no neighbor should go hungry.

Step by Step

We are with you every single step along the way!

What to Expect from our Process

  1. Contact us
    Call us or use the form below. We are ready to help you!
  2. Meet with Us
    We will meet with you in-person or over the phone. We want to hear your story. This helps us know how to help you.
  3. We make your plan
    We make a plan just for you. Your plan tells you what to do and when to do it.
  4. We help you Do your Plan
    We stay in-touch for each step of your plan. We help answer your questions and give you tools when you need them. We help you stay strong when you want to give up.
  5. You get Blessed
    We help you until your plan is done. You get the fresh start you deserve.
